The World of Disney Villains

Over 100 years of Entertainment?

A diverse cast of characters?

Complex topics explored through them?

Disney has spent the past one hundred and one years imagining up and creating complex villains that explore a range of different situations and lessons. These villains help bring forth the plots of these movies and give the audience someone to root against while exploring the ideas as to why the act the way that they do.

The evolution of Disney villains over the years has given us villains of different and diverse backgrounds. Places such as Europe, Mongolia, America, and the islands of Polynesia have all given us different villains with different motivaitons.Disney has always had a strong sense of uniquiness within all of their villains, which has been highlighted in recent years

Disney villains have long since been used to explore complex topics ranging from the harmful effects of jealousy and envy to rejection and exclusion due to differences. Disney has been using their villains as vessles for these complex topics since their inception. Many point to the hero's for these complex lessons, but in truth, many great lessons can be learned from the villains.
